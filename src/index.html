About me

I've led social media marketing for more established brands such as DreamWorks and Ford Motor Company, but have also built marketing and social media strategies from the ground up for brands like Pluto TV and Comedy Dynamics. I know the social media landscape like the back of my hand, oscillating effortlessly between paid and organic tactics.

It's my belief that the absolute best thing you can do to make a true impact as a brand on social, is to listen and respond to your audience as you would a friend. Social marketing is vastly different from traditional marketing and should be approached as such! Think of it as a party that you are crashing, but were not invited to. Nothing clears a room faster than someone trying to give a sales pitch in a room full of people just trying to have a good time. Instead, find your crowd, listen carefully, make a joke, laugh at theirs, and make a genuine connection they won’t forget. Aaand maybe slip them your business card on the way out.
